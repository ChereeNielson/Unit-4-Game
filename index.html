Drink Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four bottles below. By clicking on a bottle you will add a specific amount of shots to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number of shots, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number of shots.

The value of each bottle is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each bottle.
